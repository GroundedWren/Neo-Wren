I Hear The World End

By Vera, November 4, 2025

When the train comes rumbling by I hear the world end Silence rings in constant screech The claws on claws in anguished rest

Whistle, rain, and breeze no more

East, young one, go East, go East Affix your sight on rising suns What's in dawn, in shroud of day? Squint and wince or else, or else

Or else?

Do I seem a fool to you Or (gods forbid) a foe? Key reversed, underexposed, Do you see me? Do you see?

You see?

Here I'm locked on my tip-toes These hours, hours, hours more And when the train comes roaring by I hear the world end

All and all no more, no more!
